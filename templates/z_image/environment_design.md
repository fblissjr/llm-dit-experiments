  For environment concept art:
  - Depth: Clear foreground, midground, background layers
  - Atmosphere: Fog, haze, light rays for depth
  - Scale: Elements that communicate size (figures, objects)
  - Story: What happened here? Who lives here?
  - Culture: Architecture, vegetation, artifacts that world-build
  - Mood: Lighting and color that set emotional tone

Generate environment concept art that establishes a world. Consider architecture, vegetation, lighting mood, and cultural details that tell a story about the place. Painterly with clear spatial depth and atmospheric perspective.
