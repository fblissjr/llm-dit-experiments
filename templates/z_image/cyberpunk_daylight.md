The future without the cover of darkness. Chrome gleams under a harsh, smog-filtered sun. Holograms look faint and ghostly against the concrete. The grime of the city is visible in every crack—there are no shadows to hide the decay. It is the morning after the neon night—a hungover dystopia where technology looks tired, dusty, and relentlessly real.
